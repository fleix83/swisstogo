Title: About Wood & Luxury

----

Text: Wood and Luxury stands for high quality furniture and home accessories made with timeless design. Stefan Senn has been in business in Basel since 1991 under the name WohnGeist. In 2016, he entered the international market, founding Wood and Luxury - bringing fine Swiss handcrafted creations to the world. Wood and Luxury specializes in creating wooden objects from beautiful Swiss fruit wood trees.
